# HR Analytics: Employee Attrition Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the IBM HR Employee Attrition dataset using Python to identify factors influencing employee turnover. The analysis includes data exploration, visualization, and business insights to help HR teams improve employee retention.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition patterns.
- Explore relationships between employee attributes and attrition.
- Visualize key HR metrics.
- Generate actionable business insights.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📂 Dataset
**Dataset:** IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset

Rows: **1470**

Columns: **35**

---

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis

The project includes visualizations such as:

- Employee Attrition Distribution
- Gender Distribution
- Age Distribution
- Department-wise Employees
- Department-wise Attrition
- Job Role vs Attrition
- Overtime vs Attrition
- Monthly Income Distribution
- Monthly Income vs Attrition
- Correlation Heatmap

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Employee attrition is approximately **16%**.
- Employees working overtime have a higher attrition rate.
- Research & Development is the largest department.
- Lower monthly income is associated with higher attrition.
- Work-life balance appears to influence employee retention.
